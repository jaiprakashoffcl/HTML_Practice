LOKESH CINEMATIC UNIVERSE (LCU)



Explore LCU on Wikipedia

The Lokesh Cinematic Universe (LCU) is an Indian action-thriller franchise created by director Lokesh Kanagaraj.
It started with Kaithi (2019), followed by Vikram (2022) and Leo (2023).
The films share characters, events, and a gritty underworld setting.
